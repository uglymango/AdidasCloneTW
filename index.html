Stories, Style, and Sporting Goods at adidas, Since 1949

Through sports, we have the power to change lives. Sports keep us fit. They keep us mindful. They bring us together. Athletes inspire us. They help us to get up and get moving. And sporting goods featuring the latest technologies help us beat our personal best. adidas is home to the runner, the basketball player, the soccer kid, the fitness enthusiast, the yogi. And even the weekend hiker looking to escape the city. The 3-Stripes are everywhere and anywhere. In sports. In music. On life’s stages. Before the whistle blows, during the race, and at the finish line. We’re here to support creators. To improve their game. To live their lives. And to change the world.

Elevate Your Performance with adidas Workout Clothes

At adidas, we craft workout clothes for every athlete, from those who embrace challenges to those who redefine the game. We believe in breaking conventions, rewriting rules, and then surpassing them. Our sports apparel is engineered to empower you, inspire your victories, and help you experience life to its fullest potential. From bras and tights designed for female athletes who give it their all, to gear that offers the ultimate blend of low-to-high support and maximum comfort.

We are driven by innovation. We design, refine, and iterate, constantly testing our technologies on the field, the track, the court, and in the pool. Our inspiration stems from the nostalgia of retro workout attire as we continuously create new streetwear essentials. Styles like NMD and Ozweego, alongside our iconic Firebird tracksuits and classics like Stan Smith and Superstar, make a remarkable comeback both on the streets and stages worldwide.

Our collections blur the boundaries between high fashion and high performance. Witness this fusion in adidas by Stella McCartney's athletic clothing collection, designed to shine inside and outside the gym. Similarly, our adidas Originals lifestyle pieces seamlessly transition from sports apparel to everyday wear, reflecting our versatile lives and dynamic aspirations.

At adidas, our designs are aligned with the evolving essence of life, adapting to our ever-changing lives and aspirations.
